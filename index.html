About Spectrum

Spectrum is the flagship annual cultural festival of the National Institute of Fashion Technology (NIFT), Bhubaneswar — a vibrant celebration that brings together the brightest young minds from across the city and beyond.

More than just a college fest, Spectrum is a melting pot of creativity, talent, and expression. The festival pulses with energy across a rich and diverse array of events. From electrifying performances in the performing arts to high-spirited sports competitions, Spectrum offers every participant a stage to shine.

FAQs

1. What is Naksh? +
Naksh is the annual technical and cultural fest, featuring a blend of competitions, workshops, and entertainment nights focusing on the fusion of fashion and the cosmos.
2. When will the fest be held? +
The upcoming edition will be held from March 26-27, 2026. Keep an eye on our portal for the exact timetable.

The two day CRAFT BAZAAR, gives a good platform to artisans and people to interact and exchange design and craft knowledge and experiences.
